Thanks largely to Greg Millam there is a new release of PageTemplate. Thanks entirely to Greg Millam, it is a complete revamping of PT’s internal workings. While the basics of using PageTemplate as covered by the other pages on this site are pretty much the same, a few things have been added.

New Features

The Preprocessor

reverse

Amaze your friends! Baffle your enemies! Discover palindromes in your spare time!

This is a simple demonstration of the capabilities of PageTemplate’s preprocessor. It prints out the reversed string of the value being displayed.

Hello, [%var name%]. Your name spelled backwards is [%var name :reverse%].

Hello, Brian Wisti. Your name spelled backwards is itsiW nairB.

escapeHTML

Here comes some sample HTML escaped.

[%var html :escapeHTML %]

Here comes some sample HTML escaped.

<p><a href=’http://hack.your.email.ownzr.com/’>Update your <strong>PayPal</strong> account!</a></p>

escapeURI

[%var url :escapeURI %]

And here’s a URI-escaped string. Isn’t the preprocessor great?

Brian+Wisti+%28contact+at+brianwisti%40rubyforge.org%29
